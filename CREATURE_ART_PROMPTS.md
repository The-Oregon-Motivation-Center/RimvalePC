## STYLE ANCHOR (paste once at the start of your ChatGPT conversation)

> I'm generating a series of 118 fantasy creature portraits for a tactical RPG. Every image must be in the same consistent style:
>
> - **Single character only**, no other figures, no scenery, no background props.
> - **Full body, standing pose**, three-quarter front view, slight heroic stance.
> - **Square 1024×1024** aspect ratio.
> - **PIXEL ART style** — clean retro RPG sprite art, crisp pixels, high-resolution pixel-art character portrait (think classic JRPG sprite sheets like Octopath Traveler / Final Fantasy Tactics / SNES-era full-body portraits scaled up). Vibrant saturated colors, fine pixel detail. NOT painterly or photo-real.
> - **White or fully transparent background**, no scenery, no shadow underfoot, no environment.
> - **Centered subject** filling roughly 80% of the frame vertically.
> - **No text, no UI elements, no borders, no watermarks.**
> - When the creature is a humanoid, render their armor/clothing/weapons clearly visible.
> - When the creature has glowing magical accents, render those accents with luminous emissive coloring.
>
> I will paste each creature concept one at a time. For every one, generate exactly one image in this style. Confirm you understand and I'll send the first prompt.
